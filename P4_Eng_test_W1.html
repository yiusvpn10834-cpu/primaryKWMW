🎰 Chiikawa 風格英文作文筆記 (Letter Writing)

作文測驗範圍 (Writing Exam Scope)・Usagi yelled "YAH!" Chiikawa cheers.

「這份筆記將之前的測驗範圍（Simple Future Tense, Locations & Spots, Giving Directions）都整合進了書信範例中，圖文並茂，非常適合考前複習。筆記分為三個部分：

🎰 書信結構 (Letter Structure)

書信的各部分用Chiikawa角色標註：`Date`, `Address`, `Greeting`, `Body`, `Closing`, `Signature`。

🎰 範例書信 (Example Letter)

這封完整的信中，我用 bold 和粉色/淺藍色突出了關鍵詞，展示如何將 Simple Future Tense (will/won't), Hong Kong Spots, directions (Go out of MTR, turn right), position prepositions (opposite) 等測驗範圍完美融合到書信作文中。

範例信片段："...I will visit **The Peak** to **look at the beautiful view**. We won't watch a ballet. Then, we will visit **Ocean Park** to **ride on the cable car**. ... After that, we will **eat seafood** in **Sai Kung**."

🎰 作文小貼士 (Writing Tips)

Usagi給出建議：`Sequence Words` (First, Next, Then, After that) 和 `Common Mistakes` (`watch view` vs `look at view`, `Turn into street`)。

🏆 高分關鍵小提醒：復習之前糾正過的內容 (image_8.png)

筆記特別列出了之前功課的小失誤，提醒她：`Watch show` vs `Look at view`, 要用 `Turn into street`, 要寫 `Go straight ahead`。
